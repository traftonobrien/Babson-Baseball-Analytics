# Phase 08.1 Plan 03: Confirm-First Pitch Entry, Arsenal Filtering, and Verification

<objective>
Finish the redesigned charting workflow by making pitch entry explicit and reviewable, surfacing the always-visible totals, and reducing pitch-type clutter through arsenal-aware selection.
</objective>

## Objective
- **What:** Replace tap-to-save pitch actions with pending selection plus confirm, expose the required pitch totals, and filter pitch types by the selected pitcher's arsenal plus `Other`.
- **Why:** The current workflow is too easy to mis-tap, too noisy in its pitch-type choices, and too weak in the inning/pitch-count context the staff needs.
- **Output:** A safer pitch-entry workflow with clearer information density and verification coverage.
