# Phase 9 Plan 01: Restore Intentional Pilot Shell

<objective>
Make the iPad shell behave like a real internal beta by restoring sign-in gating, surfacing build identity, and making the app's operational context visible to testers.
</objective>

## Objective
- **What:** Remove the current auth bypass, restore real login gating, and expose build/session/server details in the app shell.
- **Why:** A pilot cannot be trusted if the internal build hides authentication behavior or leaves testers unable to report what build and environment they are using.
- **Output:** A deliberate beta shell that opens through login, shows version/build metadata, and supports controlled operator sign-out.
