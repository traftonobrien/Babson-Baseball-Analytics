# Phase 9 Plan 02: Harden Sync Diagnostics and Recovery

<objective>
Close the remaining pilot risk in the operational layer by making snapshot sync durable, revision-safe, and visible enough for staff to recover quickly when something fails.
</objective>

## Objective
- **What:** Fix the iPad-to-server snapshot sync contract, adopt server revisions after successful syncs, and add structured diagnostics/retry tools in the app.
- **Why:** TestFlight readiness is meaningless if the pilot build cannot actually persist the charted game or help staff recover from sync issues.
- **Output:** A reliable snapshot sync path plus operator-facing diagnostics, retry actions, and recovery guidance.
